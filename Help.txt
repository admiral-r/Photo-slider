after downloading the project, copy all your photos to the "pic" folder
Photos must be in jpg format
Select all the photos with ctrl+A keys
Then right-click on a photo and rename it to "pic"
After doing this, all your photos should have the format "pic (1) .jpg" in order.
Finally, open the "script-slider.js" file with the Notepad and replace the "Photos" value with the number of your photos
